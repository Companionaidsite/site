About us

Our project is based on the ideas and values of feminism. «Companion» - is a place of solidarity and the significance of individual stories. We want to help find their voice for those who are still silent. And maybe make the world a bit safer.

Our values

Engagement and mutual support

Preventing violence

Tolerance, non-discrimination

Responsibility for one's own security

International awareness and solidarity

Care for ourselves and others

What we do

Blog about unsafe places with a map

We collect your stories about unsafe places in cities. It's not just a collection of information, but a step towards making a difference. We create a map to avoid unsafe routes and emphasize safety issues in cities.

There is a link to add your story

Companion «Community»

Our community is a safe place. In our Telegram -chat room* you can tell your stories, share information, and find support, comrades, travel partners, and friends. Our dedicated bot assigns ratings to members and helps build safer connections.

*To ensure maximum safety in the chat room, each member undergoes strict verification

Become part of the community

The «Companion» app

In our app, you can share your location and send out information in case of danger to other female users or our 24/7 support center.
